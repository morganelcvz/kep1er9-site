Girls Planet 999

01. Girls Planet 999

Girls Planet 999 was a survival show that broadcasted on Mnet. The show's first episode broadcasted on Friday, August 6, 2021 at 8:20 PM (KST) and ran every Friday with 12 episodes in total. The final episode aired on October 22, 2021 where the final top 9 contestants were announced.
Girls Planet 999 was based on the themes and aesthetics of space where each of the three groups (Korea, China, Japan) represent a 'Planet'. In addition to the three 'Planets', there was a 'Master Council' made up of the two 'K-pop Masters' whose roles were to mentor the 99 girls, four 'Vocal/Dance Masters' who trained the contestants' vocal, rap, and dance skills, and the 'Planet Master' who was the MC with the role of connecting the viewers with the show.
Outside of the 'Master Council' were the fans and audience, also known as the 'Planet Guardians', who had the role of determining the ranks of each contestant and ultimately the outcome of Girls Planet 999. The mobile app and web platform 'UNIVERSE' had the purpose of connecting the contestants and the 'Planet Guardians' through voting and other forms of media (such as video clips and photos featuring the contestants).

02. Kep1er

Kep1er (케플러) is a 7-member K-pop project girl group managed by WAKEONE Entertainment and SWING Entertainment.
The group consists of Yujin, Xiaoting, Chaehyun, Dayeon, Hikaru, Huening Bahiyyih, and Youngeun. On September 30, 2024, the group signed a management contract with KLAP Entertainment.
The group was former through the Mnet survival show Girls Planet 999. The final lineup was announced on October 22, 2021, during the final episode of Girls Planet 999. They were set to promote for 2 years and 6 months starting on their debut date. Kep1er officially debuted on January 3, 2022, with their first mini album, FIRST IMPACT. The group made their Japanese debut on September 7, 2022. Ahead of their contracts expiration, it was announced that 7 members decided to extend their contracts. The group continued as 7 members after “Kep1going On” promotions, as Mashiro and Yeseo did not renew. On July 4, 2024, Mashiro and Yeseo’s contacts officially ended, but they concluded their activities with the group after their scheduled Japanese concert, on July 15, 2024.

01. Kep1er Website

Kep1Going On is a fanmade project for a fictitious website to present the 9 members of the band Kep1er during their first and last album called 'Kep1going On'. This album marks the end of the band's activities as a 9-member group. The site exists to promote the songs and activities of the Kep1er band during the promotional period of Kep1Going On. It is a non-profit site. Above all, it pays tribute to the 9 members of the band when they were still together.
