Bio
Choi Yujin is a former member of the CLC group, formerly under the Cube Entertainment agency. Her activities in the CLC group lasted from 2015 to 2021 before she took part in the Girls Planet 999 show as the oldest contestant.
Yujin was announced as the number 3 member of Kep1er during the show's final. Yujin was named Group Leader and has been active from 2022 to the present day.
Yujin knows how to speak Japanese and has already acted in Korean dramas.

Bio
Mashiro is a Japanese idol who was formerly a trainee at JYP Entertainment from 2016 to 2018 before moving to Pledis Entertainment from 2019 to 2020 and finally 143 Entertainment in 2021. She then took part in the Girls Planet 999 show and won eighth place in the Kep1er group.
Mashiro is an active member of Kep1er from 2022 to 2024. Following the end of her contract, she returned to 143 Entertainment to join the MADEIN group as Leader.

Bio
Chaehyun is a former trainee at SM Entrainement, where she trained for 4 years before signing up as an individual trainee for the Girls Planet 999 program.
Chaehyun took first place in the final, making her the center of the Kep1er group. In addition to this role, Chaehyun is renowned for her vocal skills and is also a main vocalist.
Chaehyun has been active since 2022 and is now with WAKEONE Entertainment.

Bio
Yeseo is an Idol at 143 Entertainment. Yeseo is the youngest member of the Kep1er group, which came sixth in the Girls Planet 999 final. She is also the member who made her debut at a very young age: having been part of the group CutieL during her childhood before joining the group Busters in 2019 at the age of 14.
Yeseo left the Kep1er group in 2024 following the end of her contract. She is currently a member of MADEIN with Mashiro, a member who shares the same agency.
